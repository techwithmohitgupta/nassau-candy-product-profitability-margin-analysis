## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Product Line Profitability & Margin Performance Analysis Dashboard** built for **Nassau Candy Distributor**.

The dashboard helps analyze product-level profitability, margin performance, cost efficiency, division-level performance, and profit concentration across the product portfolio.

It is designed to support executive decision-making by answering important profitability questions:

- Which products are generating the highest profit?
- Which products are below the selected margin threshold?
- Which divisions are performing strongly or weakly?
- Which products carry high cost or margin risk?
- How concentrated is profit across the portfolio?
- Is the business dependent on only a few profit-driving products?

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Nassau Candy Distributor needs a clear way to monitor product profitability and margin performance across different product lines and divisions.

Without a dynamic dashboard, it becomes difficult to identify:

- Low-margin products
- Cost-heavy products
- Division-level profitability gaps
- Profit dependency risk
- Products driving most of the portfolio profit
- Products that require pricing, cost, or portfolio-level attention

This dashboard solves that problem by converting raw product, cost, sales, and profit data into a dynamic executive analytics system.

## 🧩 Dashboard Modules

The dashboard is divided into four major analytical modules.

---

### 1️⃣ Product Profitability Overview

This module focuses on product-level profit contribution and margin performance.

**Key Features**

- Product-level profit contribution chart
- Profit contribution share donut
- Product-level margin leaderboard
- Selected product vs remaining portfolio comparison
- Dynamic product count
- Top profit product
- Average product margin
- Margin status based on selected threshold

**Business Value**

This section helps identify which products are driving profitability and which products require attention due to weak margin performance.

---

### 2️⃣ Division Performance Dashboard

This module compares performance across divisions.

**Key Features**

- Division-level revenue and profit comparison
- Gross margin by division
- Margin distribution by division
- Revenue contribution percentage
- Profit contribution percentage
- Division performance summary table

**Business Value**

This section helps business users understand which divisions are strongest, weakest, and most important to total portfolio performance.

---

### 3️⃣ Cost vs Margin Diagnostics

This module identifies cost-heavy and margin-risk products.

**Key Features**

- Cost-to-sales ratio
- Gross margin percentage
- Profit per unit
- Margin risk flag
- High cost load detection
- Profit risk detection
- Cost diagnostic product table
- Cost vs sales scatter analysis
- Margin risk flag comparison

**Business Value**

This section helps identify products where high costs or low margins may be reducing profitability.

---

### 4️⃣ Profit Concentration Analysis

This module applies Pareto-style analysis to understand portfolio dependency.

**Key Features**

- Pareto profit concentration chart
- Cumulative profit percentage
- Core 80% profit drivers
- Long-tail product identification
- Dependency risk indicators
- Top 1, Top 3, Top 5 contribution view
- Product dependency status
- Profit concentration product table

**Business Value**

This section helps determine whether the business is too dependent on a small group of products for most of its profit.

---

## 🎛️ Dynamic Filters

Every KPI, chart, table, and insight updates dynamically based on the selected filters.

| Filter | Purpose |
|---|---|
| 📅 Date Range | Analyze performance within a selected time period |
| 🏢 Division | Filter dashboard by product division |
| 🍬 Product Search | Analyze all products or a selected product |
| 📉 Margin Threshold (%) | Set custom margin benchmark for risk detection |

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators

The dashboard includes an executive KPI row with the following metrics:

| KPI | Formula / Meaning |
|---|---|
| Gross Margin (%) | Gross Profit ÷ Sales |
| Profit per Unit | Gross Profit ÷ Units |
| Revenue Contribution | Filtered Sales ÷ Total Sales |
| Profit Contribution | Filtered Profit ÷ Total Profit |
| Margin Volatility | Variability of margin over time |

---

## 🚨 Margin Threshold Risk Monitor

This section helps identify product risk based on the selected margin threshold.

| Risk Metric | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Products Below Threshold | Number of products below selected margin benchmark |
| Revenue at Risk | Revenue generated by below-threshold products |
| Profit at Risk | Profit generated by below-threshold products |
| Selected Threshold | Current margin benchmark selected by user |
